KS Apparel is a Fashion and Lifestyle wholesale retailer procuring materials from various manufacturers. Since it is a very competitive market, there are several competing companies. From past couple of years, SR Apparel has been losing contracts regularly and the contracts gained are not profitable.

Hari, the CEO of SR Apparel was worried due to the declining profits. He suspects that there is a element of variability in the demand that is affecting his profits. After last month's discussion with Sameer, the technology whiz-kid, he was promised a simulation tool that will help his team visualize the demands and compare the contracts.


Instructions to use the tool

  • To simulate and compare multiple contract types, click on Compare tab.
  • Select multiple contract types you would like to verify.
  • Enter the parameters.
  • Click on Run Simulation.
  • You can check/uncheck the result variables for better clarity.
